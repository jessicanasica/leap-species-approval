About WETSAPP

WETSAPP is a wetland monitoring application developed by Nature Seychelles as part of the LEAP Wetland Monitoring Programme. It enables field teams, conservation partners, and school clubs to record biodiversity observations, health assessments, pollution reports, and cleanup activities across Seychelles' wetland sites.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access to the app?

WETSAPP is an unlisted app available to authorised members of the Nature Seychelles wetland monitoring programme. If you are a field team member, conservation partner, or school club participant, please contact Nature Seychelles to request access and receive your login credentials.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Please contact us at corinne@natureseychelles.org and we will help you reset your account credentials.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. WETSAPP is designed for field use and works fully offline. All data you enter while offline is stored securely on your device and automatically uploaded when an internet connection becomes available.

My data isn't syncing. What should I do?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection, then open the app and navigate to the home page — the app will attempt to sync automatically. If the problem persists, try logging out and logging back in, or contact us for assistance.

How do I submit a monitoring report?

From the home screen, select the type of mission you want to carry out (Biodiversity, Health, or Threats). Follow the on-screen prompts to select a location, fill in the form, and submit your survey. You can also save forms as drafts and complete them later.

What access levels are available?

WETSAPP has four access levels:

  • Level 1 — School Club: Basic monitoring and observation features.
  • Level 2 — Conservation Partner: Extended data collection capabilities.
  • Level 3 — Superuser: Advanced features including species management.
  • Level 4 — Admin: Full access including data management and user administration.

You can request a higher access level from within the app on the home page.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Please email us at corinne@natureseychelles.org with a description of the issue or your suggestion. Include screenshots if possible.

How is my data used?

All monitoring data is used exclusively for conservation research and management by the Nature Seychelles Wetland Monitoring Programme. We do not sell or share your personal information. For full details, please see our Privacy Policy.
