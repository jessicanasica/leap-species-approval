WETSAPP Privacy Policy

Wetland Monitoring Application by Nature Seychelles

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

WETSAPP ("the App") is a wetland monitoring application developed as part of the Nature Seychelles Wetland Monitoring Programme, in collaboration with the LEAP project. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information to support wetland monitoring activities:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address, used for authentication and identifying data submissions.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates captured when recording field observations (flora/fauna surveys, health assessments, pollution reports, etc.). Location is only collected when you actively use the App's monitoring features.
  • Photos: Images captured through the App's camera for documenting environmental conditions, pollution incidents, and biodiversity observations.
  • Environmental Monitoring Data: Field observations including species counts, health assessments, water quality measurements, cleanup activity records, biomass measurements, and other ecological data you enter into the App.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for managing offline data synchronisation.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Recording and storing wetland monitoring data for conservation research and management.
  • Generating environmental reports and statistics for the Nature Seychelles Wetland Monitoring Programme.
  • Synchronising your offline data with our servers when an internet connection is available.
  • Identifying data submissions by field team members.

4. Data Storage and Security

Monitoring data is stored locally on your device when offline and synchronised to our secure servers (hosted on Supabase) when connectivity is available. All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS. Access to the data is restricted to authorised members of the Nature Seychelles monitoring team.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Environmental monitoring data may be used in aggregated, anonymised form for scientific publications, conservation reports, and programme evaluations by Nature Seychelles and its partners.

6. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library solely for capturing and attaching photos to monitoring reports. Photos are stored locally and uploaded to our servers as part of your field reports. We do not access your personal photos outside of the App.

7. Location Services

The App uses location services ("When In Use") to record GPS coordinates for monitoring sites. Location data is only captured when you actively submit a monitoring report or record a new site. The App does not track your location in the background.

8. Offline Data

The App is designed to work offline. Data entered while offline is stored securely on your device and queued for upload when an internet connection becomes available. You can review pending uploads within the App.

9. Data Retention

Monitoring data is retained for the duration of the Nature Seychelles Wetland Monitoring Programme. You may request deletion of your personal account information by contacting us at the email address below.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by authorised field team members and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
